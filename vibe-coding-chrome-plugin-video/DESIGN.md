## Style Prompt

Dark, polished fintech developer demo. The visuals should feel like a practical product walkthrough: deep blue-black canvas, frosted panels, teal market accents, Binance-like yellow highlights, crisp code surfaces, and screenshots treated as the proof of the build. Motion is fast, confident, and readable.

## Colors

- Background: #07101a
- Surface: #111b28
- Elevated surface: #1a2534
- Text: #f4f7fb
- Muted text: #9aa8bb
- Accent teal: #38e3bb
- Accent cyan: #21c6d6
- Market yellow: #ffd34d
- Down red: #ff6d80

## Typography

- UI / headings: Inter, SF Pro Display, system-ui, sans-serif
- Code: JetBrains Mono, SFMono-Regular, Consolas, monospace

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use generic purple-blue gradients.
- Do not let screenshots sit flat without context or motion.
- Do not over-explain with dense paragraphs.
- Do not use light marketing-page styling.
